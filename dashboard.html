About Autofluorescence Detection

OralScan AI uses advanced autofluorescence imaging technology combined with deep learning to detect early signs of oral cancer. When oral tissue is exposed to blue light (400-460nm), healthy tissue fluoresces bright green due to collagen and elastin content, while abnormal tissue shows reduced or dark fluorescence.

Our AI model has been trained on thousands of clinical images to identify suspicious patterns with high accuracy, helping clinicians make informed decisions for early intervention.
